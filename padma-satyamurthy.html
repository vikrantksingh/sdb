  • Padma Satyamurthy

Padma Satyamurthy

Senior leader with 25 years of experience in both Fortune 50 companies as well as start ups / unicorns in diverse leadership roles across delivery, operation and strategy. My core competencies are in the areas of problem solving, overcoming complex business challenges, working in high-pressure goal driven environments with strong sense of judgement and ethics and integrity. Apart from leading large business groups for growth, I have been an entrepreneur myself with a consulting and transformation start up where I worked with clients like Philips Healthcare, ABB Ltd, Honeywell Technologies etc helping them with their complex transformation challenges for new business growth through innovation and product development. I have worked extensively in new product development development in Concept to Cash model with my clients.

Multi domain expertise in retail, healthcare, BFSI and industrial automation. Expertise in business and digital transformation, delivery leadership, strategic business development and product / portfolio planning. Over the last decade, I have been deeply engaged in large complex transformation through driving changes in culture, people, technology and capabilities as well as operating models.

I am passionate about startup ecosystem and am a mentor and expert advisor on accelerator programs including Mass Challenge Israel and Boston.

In my last role as Director at Cprime, I was driving on building new market space in Asia and Europe by adding new clients . I was also involved in adding new capabilities to the existing consulting offerings that helps expand our market penetration.

I am a regular speaker at international forums at US, The Netherlands, Malaysia, Turkey etc and have shared my experience on diverse topics of leadership, transformation, talent and culture. I am also part of various leadership forums specially focusing on women in leadership space.

Currently, I am working at Walmart IDC focusing on transformation across the organization adopting agile and devops practices. The goal of the transformation is to build high-end technology solutions that drives business growth and outcome.
