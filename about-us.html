Welcome to Scrum Day Bangalore Conference

Bengaluru or Bangalore is the IT Capital and Silicon Valley of India. Scrum is the most popular Agile Framework. Xebia is the leading Agile Consulting and Training firm. When these three come together – Its festival of knowledge and networking. Xebia proudly presents Scrum Day Bangalore 2021 – the festival of knowledge and networking. Its time for Agile Enthusiasts, Agile Practitioners all over the world to visit namma Bengaluru virtually to come together, share the knowledge and network. With an awesome, most pressing need of the hour theme “Metrics and Measurements – Mirrors of Enterprise Mental Models” discover, explore, deep dive into the madness behind metrics – the good, bad and ugly of number crunching.

Why Choose Scrum Day?

Scrum day - Bangalore is a forum for and by the Agility explorers and caretakers of Scrum to have a meaningful conversation about the pragmatic way of adopting Scrum in various contexts and business domains. Scrum DNA is rooted in Complex Adaptive Systems (CAS) theory and thrives on relationship patterns among elements. Scrum is a framework which provides a shared space for emerging relationships" to deliver a valuable product to the customers. Scrum Guide is the rule book. To understand its rules better and learn new strategies, the Scrum community should come together and share their experiential knowledge!
  • A platform for all the Agile practitioners across the globe to collaborate.
  • Focus on wonderful theme "Metrics and Measurements: Mirrors of enterprise Mental Models".
  • Measuring the right metrics strengthens the structure of an enterprise to create an engaging environment for the creative people and nurtures the collective intelligence to solve wicked problems.
  • Let's learn from the insights of world-class Agile thought leaders and co-actively explore the diverse perspectives about metrics.
